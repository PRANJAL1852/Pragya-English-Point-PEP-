About Us

Pragya English Point (PEP) is the leading coaching center for Academic English, Competitive English, and Spoken English. Established in 1995, we have been empowering students with excellent English skills and helping them achieve their academic and professional goals. Our coaching center is open until 8 PM to accommodate students' schedules.

We offer a wide range of courses, including:

  • Spoken English (Basic & Advanced)
  • Competitive English (SSC, Bank, Air Force, etc.)
  • M.A. & B.A. Classes (Hindi & English)
  • TGT, PGT, NET, JRF Preparation
  • CUET, CTET, UPTET, SUPER TET Coaching

Join us today and take the first step towards a brighter future!
