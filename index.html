About Us

Pragya English Point (PEP) is the leading coaching center for Academic English, Competitive English, and Spoken English. Our mission is to empower students with excellent English skills and help them achieve their academic and professional goals. With a team of experienced faculty members, we provide personalized coaching to ensure every student reaches their full potential.

Established in 2020, PEP has helped over 1,000 students achieve success in various competitive exams, including SSC, Bank, Air Force, and more. We offer a wide range of courses, including Spoken English, Competitive English, M.A. & B.A. classes, and preparation for TGT, PGT, NET, JRF, CUET, CTET, UPTET, and SUPER TET.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and innovative teaching methods make us the best choice for English coaching in Rasra, Uttar Pradesh. Join us today and take the first step towards a brighter future!
